IKARUGA―――Alarm


WARNING
（On-Marishi-Ei-Sowaka）
The big enemy is approaching
at full throttle.
According to the data. It is
identified as "Butsutekkai"
NO REFUGE

　The Sanskrit word "On-Marishi-Ei-Sowaka" is a spell of "Marishiten" who is the goddess of shimmering air. The spell has a power to escape every disaster.

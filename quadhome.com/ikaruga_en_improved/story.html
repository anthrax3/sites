IKARUGA———Story

Hourai was once a small nation at the end of the mainland. But now, this nation calls itself Jintuusya. Wielding the the power of God, Hourai conquers other nations through military power.

In the beginning, Stone-like was dug up from the ground by Hourai-Tenrou. Hourai-Tenrou is the ruler of Hourai. Hourai-Tenrou has performed miracles since she encountered Stone-like.

Opposing Hourai, there was Tenkaku, a resistance group defending liberty and resisting Hourai. They use fighter planes called Hitekkai and fight against Hourai. But the Tenkaku lost their power and were annihilated.

However, miraculously one young man named Shinra survived.

Shinra fought against Hourai again by himself, but he was shot down and crashed at the remote village of Ikaruga. The village is composed of old people, ignored and renounced1 by Hourai. Shinra was helped by old Kazamori and the other old people.

Shinra recovered completely and informed the villagers that he would fight against Hourai again. The villagers gave the Ikaruga to Shinra.

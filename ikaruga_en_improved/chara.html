◆SHINRA

　Once, he was a member of "TENKAKU." When he was defeated by "HOURAI" and fell at a village of Ikaruga, he was saved by the old of the village. After that, he was left with "IKARUGA" and challenged "HOURAI" to fight again. His wish is to be set at liberty from the control of "HOURAI." But he is a little rash, because of his purpose to go through with it not to regret in life.


◆KAGARI

　She was once a people of "HOURAI" and an assassin to kill Shinra. But when she was defeated by Shinra and crashed, Shinra helped her. Immediately after when she was helped, her wish was suicide because of her noble personality. However, as days go by, she recovered her presence of mind. At present, she lives in a village of Ikaruga, because she want see the "Liberty" that Shinra and the old talk.


◇IKARUGA

　"IKARUGA" is "Hitekkai" what was maked by Amanai who was a wonderful technician, old Kazamori, and a village headman Shinkai. IKARUGA is the first "Hitekkai" what has power of both "Yin" and "Yan." It is hidden underground at a village of Ikaruga. When sallying out, forwarding machine "Hudoumyouou-no-Ken" forward "IKARUGA" to out.

This is Sam's translation of the weapon damage tables--long range only for right now--from The Book of Cyber Space Divers (© SEGA 2000-2001). Before digging in, to the data, there are a few translator's notes.

Not all the data is necassarily accurate. This is all taken DIRECTLY from the book, WITHOUT cross referencing it. If there are errors, please don't email me with corrections.

All the damage is taken from 200 meters against Temjin with 0 V-armour.

For the most part, the translation is direct, without too much over localization. I tried to keep it as simple as possible.

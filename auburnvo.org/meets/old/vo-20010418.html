I GET TO SLEEP TONIGHT

You don't understand what I've been going through for the past couple weeks. Thus, I'm going to explain it to you.

For two weeks, I couldn't sleep...

I couldn't sleep. I couldn't sleep. Gah! This, obviously, has been affecting my school life. Primarily, I haven't started on my English homework. Heh.

When you're in Fight Club...

It's also been affecting my VOOT practice. Mainly, my practice is worth crap because I don't get any retention. You practice, get a good nights sleep, and it all stays. This isn't working out for me. It showed tonight. I held my own; however, I wasn't performed half as well as I usually do. We got a few replays though. Mark should be posting them on VOA sometime tonight.

And come tournament?

I'll be ready. Hopefully you will be too. Check out the AVC Homepage for the next warm-up gathering. You know you want to place mad beat-down, or just learn how.

LET'S GET READY TO RUMBLE

You heard me! Go look on the main site. If you don't know, now you know. Sakura Con is coming and AVC is running a multi-state VOOT tournament there.
 
Most of us haven't done any serious gameplay for a few weeks, and in some situation a few months. However, we want to play our hardest and make the best showing we possibly can. Do your best.

So how do I do that?

Simple. Right up until the tournament, Matt is hosting Wednesday gatherings starting at 7:30pm. You attend, you play, you rock.

But I can't get there...

If you need any help in attending: transportation issues, timing problems, lack of confidence, or just plain need to hear my soothing sexy voice, give me a call and we'll see what we can arrange. I've been known to make home visits.
 
We'll be waiting.

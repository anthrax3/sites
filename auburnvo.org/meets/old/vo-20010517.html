The Pat Question

Nothing but bad news and worse rumours. Brent spoke to Pat and she didn't sound too keen about coming over and playing some VO with us... for a variety of reasons. Not the ones you're all thinking though. Be nice.
 
In the worse rumours category, Brent said Pat was thinking about taking down her hardcore VO site. She's been getting a bit tired of the "fan-mail" for years now, and Brent thinks she might be getting tired of VO as well. All of the above is a darn frick'n shame. Especially given Pat single-handedly created many VO scenes, if by her helpful e-mail or her infamous guide to VOOM. DO NOT go out and e-mail her about all this... I don't think it would help anything.
 
Anyway, that's a heads up. Maybe someone should mirror her site?

Why the time change?

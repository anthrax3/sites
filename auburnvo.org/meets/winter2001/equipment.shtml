VCR Requirements

All VCRs must have both an RCA in and out abilities as well as be able to record the signal passing through. EP or LP recording abilities are also a must.

Camcorder Requirements

Camcorders used must either be able to record directly to a VHS tape, or have a capturable digital out. A purely digital camcorder would be best, but I don't think we have access to one.

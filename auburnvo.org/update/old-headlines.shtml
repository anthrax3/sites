Headline: Wed Jan 16 13:53:40 PST 2002
POSTED BY: Scott
---
One full year of AVC-Seattle. It started with a bang, and definately ended with one too.

I never expected to meet so many people or to have so much fun from this wild and crazy trip. A lot of people have learned to love the game, and others have decided to take an "exented hiatus".

Amazingly, through it all almost everyone has managed to stay friends and no evil politicing has reared its ugly head. We have our own domain name and multiple sets of twinsticks, DCs, and even our own 5.4g arcade site! I don't think anyone could have predicted that at the beginning of 2001.

Happy New Year everyone, if I wasn't able to wish it to you in-person. I look forward to enjoying another one with everyone - VO or not.
